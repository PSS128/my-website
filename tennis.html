Tennis Coaching

Tennis has been a big part of my life, and coaching gives me the chance to share that with others. I work with players of all levels — from beginners learning to rally to competitive juniors looking to sharpen their game.

What I Offer

Beginner Lessons

Fundamentals-first approach: grip, footwork, groundstrokes, and court awareness. Great for adults and kids picking up the sport for the first time.

Intermediate Development

Consistency, point construction, and shot selection. For players who can rally and want to compete more effectively.

Match Preparation

Strategy, mental game, and situational drilling. Focused on players preparing for tournaments, leagues, or school tryouts.

My Background

I've played tennis competitively since high school and carried that love for the game through university. At UC Santa Barbara I was part of the club tennis team and eventually led it — it was one of the best experiences I had in university, and I bring that same passion to coaching. It was a genuinely tight-knit community and I'm grateful to have been a part of it.

I focus on building a player's confidence alongside their technique — the mental side of the game matters just as much as the physical. Whether you're picking up a racket for the first time or prepping for competition, the goal is always the same: enjoy the process and get better.
